Introduction

The current focus of the Mathews are two main tributaries of the Spokane River. The two tributaries are the Little Spokane River and Hangman Creek. The Little Spokane River begin North of Spokane in the Mountains and flows south until its confluence with the Spokane River. Conversely, Hangman Creek begins south of Spokane, in the Rocky Mountains of Idaho. Hangman Creek flows northwest until its confluence with the Spokane River near downtown Spokane.
